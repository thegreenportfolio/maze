Learning Maze – Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 27, 2026

1. Introduction

Learning Maze (“the App”) is a maze-based educational game that combines exploration with short knowledge questions. We designed it to be simple, fun, and respectful of your privacy.

2. Data We Collect

Learning Maze does not collect personal data. The App does not require account creation, sign-in, or user identification.

All gameplay content is available offline, and no analytics or advertising SDKs are used.

3. In-App Purchases

Learning Maze offers an optional, one-time, non-consumable in-app purchase to unlock the full game. All payments are processed securely by Apple via the App Store.

We do not receive or store payment information, billing details, or Apple ID data.

4. Game Data

Game progress (such as completed levels, selected topics, difficulty settings, and preferences) is stored locally on your device. This data is not transmitted off the device.

You can remove all locally stored data at any time by deleting the App.

5. Children’s Privacy

Learning Maze is suitable for a general audience. The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if the App changes. Any updates will be published at this same URL.
